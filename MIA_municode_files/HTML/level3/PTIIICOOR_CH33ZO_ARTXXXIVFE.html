Sec. 33-291. Exemption from fees.

The Board of Public Instruction, the County of Miami-Dade, the State of Florida, the United States of America, and all corporate municipalities situated wholly within the boundaries of the County, shall be exempted from the payment of any fee for a building permit or a certificate of use and occupancy where the work is done wholly by personnel of any such agency; where the work done for such agency is let on contract, the contractor shall secure the necessary permits and pay the required fees; where the work is to be done solely by personnel of the governmental agency, the permit shall be issued in the name of such agency upon application for any building construction, occupancy or usage, not contrary to the provisions of this chapter.

(Ord. No. 57-19, § 40, 10-22-57)
