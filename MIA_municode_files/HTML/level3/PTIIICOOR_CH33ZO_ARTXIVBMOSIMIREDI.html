ARTICLE XIVB. RU-1M(a), MODIFIED SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL DISTRICT

Sec. 33-200.3. Uses permitted.


Sec. 33-200.3. Uses permitted.

No land, body of water and/or structure shall be used or permitted to be used and no structure shall be hereafter erected, constructed, moved, reconstructed or structurally altered for any purpose in an RU-1M(a) District which is designed, arranged, or intended to be used or occupied for any purpose other than those uses specifically provided in the RU-1 District.
