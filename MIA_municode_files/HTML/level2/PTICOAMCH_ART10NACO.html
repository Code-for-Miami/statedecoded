ARTICLE 10. NAME OF COUNTY

SECTION 10.01. NAME OF COUNTY.


SECTION 10.01. NAME OF COUNTY.

A.

The name of Dade County shall officially be changed to Miami-Dade County and all references to Dade County in the Florida Constitution, Florida Statutes, Code of Metropolitan Dade County, federal law, case law and other legal documents, shall be deemed to be references to Miami-Dade County.

B.

The Commission shall by ordinance provide a method to implement the official name change.

Note: Miami-Dade County Ordinance 97-212. This ordinance is codified in Section 1-4.2 in the Code of Ordinances and is recorded in the Official Records of Miami-Dade County, Florida at Book 17968, Page 0498.
